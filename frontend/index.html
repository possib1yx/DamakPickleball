Got Questions?

We've got answers.

Do I need to bring my own paddle and balls?

No, we provide premium paddles and balls for free with every court rental!

How many people can play on one court?

A standard booking is for up to 4 players (doubles). If you have a larger group and want to rotate players, please let us know in the booking message.

Is the court indoor or outdoor?

We have premium outdoor courts. Enjoy playing in the fresh air under the open sky. The lighting is professional grade.

How do I pay?

Currently, we accept payment at the venue via Cash or eSewa/Khalti QR scan before your game starts.
